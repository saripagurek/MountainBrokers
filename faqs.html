FAQs

In a company of your size, what is your attitude towards your employees?

-J.B

We find that treating our employees with respect helps keep our company moving. Acknowledging their achievements,and appealing to their human sides. Often large corporations get lost, and treat their workers like machines,but we think a comfortable work environment will make the workers feel even happier about the work they are doing. Our building is filled with comfortable chairs, and quiet places to work. White boards to encourage creative ideas, and snacks to keep them going throughout the day.

-Sari Pagurek (Founder)

How did your company grow to be so large and successful?

-Anonymous

That is a great question, and we will gladly answer it for you. You need to understand the concept of ‘successful’ to become a fully functioning company. Let us tell you, we are definitely a large and successful company, that much is clear. But as to how we became so successful we really looked deep enough to see our competitors’ successes and learned and built from there. Our success is the foundation of our company and was obtained, successfully building our business.

-Alyssa Carter (Founder)
